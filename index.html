What is Le Tunnel?

Le Tunnel is a french collective of two students that will create, plan and run festive electronic music events in underground urban areas. Our activity will combine two main disciplines which are Urbex and Techno parties. Through our univers, we want to offer a unique visual and auditive experience to our customers while working closely with local talented artist that need more recognition. We plan to launch the project by the end of 2020. Like our name suggests, the events will take place in small underground areas such as tunnels or forsaken places as long as they are safe. We will make our best to make Le Tunnel a great journey with regular events through the year, with an aim at one event each month. Each event will be an intimate and colorful experience while displaying the unique identity of our collective.
